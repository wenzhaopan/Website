Classic game of pong coded using Java Object Oriented Programming. Specifically, with JPanel and JFrame.

2 Players can play this game. Each player tries to hit the ball with thier paddle back to the other player. Score a point by making the other player miss the ball! First to 5 points wins. But beware, the ball moves ever faster the more you rally!




Connect-4 with a twist! This was coded using Java Object Oriented Programming. Specifically, with JPanel and JFrame.

In this game, you can play against another player or against a computer program. The twist is that there are powerups! The blue tile puts a piece of your colour in the adjacent columns and the green tile skips the opponent's next turn.







Text-based game based on the popular TV show "Who Wants To Be A Millionaire". This project was coded in Java.

Complete with two lifelines: "50/50" lifeline where two wrong answers will be eliminated and the "ask the audience" lifeline where the audience will vote for what they think the right answer is.
